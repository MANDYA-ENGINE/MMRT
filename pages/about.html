About MMRT

Our Mission

Madhura Mandya Rangakala Trust (MMRT) is dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Mandya through performing arts, theatre, and cultural activities. We strive to create a platform for local artists and nurture young talent in the field of performing arts.

Our Vision

To be the leading cultural institution in Mandya, fostering artistic excellence and cultural awareness while preserving our traditional art forms for future generations.

Our Values

  • Cultural Preservation
  • Artistic Excellence
  • Community Engagement
  • Educational Outreach
  • Innovation in Traditional Arts
